What's New?

A thank you from the owner:

When we moved from New York to Raleigh, we couldn't find any decent bubble tea in the area to satisfy our cravings. Though inexperienced and unsure, we took a chance and decided to open our own café.

On May 20th, 2016, Quickly Café in RTP opened its doors. With no advertisements at all, to our surprise, people were lined up at the door to give us a try.

We've learned a lot in our first year of business, and though we've made great strides, there will always be room to improve.

To all of our customers, thank you from the bottom of our hearts for giving us a chance. We hope to provide you with quality drinks and excellent service for years to come!

- Sunny
